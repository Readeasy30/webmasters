# RestaurantAIbot Canva Graphics Queue

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

This file gives Canva-ready graphic prompts for the RestaurantAIbot owner-resource campaign.

Use these prompts to create reusable social graphics, Pinterest pins, video covers, and simple campaign images.

Primary landing page:

`https://restaurantaibot.com/restaurant-owner-resources.html`

## Design Direction

Brand feel:

- warm local restaurant
- practical business help
- trustworthy
- mobile-readable
- not flashy
- not corporate cold

Suggested colors:

- warm orange
- cream or white background
- dark charcoal text
- subtle restaurant imagery

Text rules:

- large headline
- short subheadline
- visible footer: `RestaurantAIBot.com`
- readable on a phone
- avoid tiny checklist text
