## Daily Publishing Pattern

Use this simple rhythm when content starts going live:

1. Publish or improve one helpful page.
2. Create one matching Canva graphic.
3. Create one short social post.
4. Add one internal link from another page.
5. Log the work in project status.

## Weekly Review Questions

Ask these every Sunday:

1. Did we publish helpful content or just create files?
2. Did every health/safety page include vet guidance?
3. Did we avoid unsupported diagnosis/treatment claims?
4. Did we create matching social/Canva assets?
5. Did we move closer to traffic, email signup, or monetization readiness?

## Do Not Do Yet

- Do not add live affiliate links until disclosure and product standards are ready.
- Do not add paid local sponsor listings until disclosure and listing rules are ready.
- Do not add public AI pet-health answers without strict safety limits.
- Do not add payment collection on PetNeeds.ai.
- Do not add intrusive ads to emergency/safety pages.
