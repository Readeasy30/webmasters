## Publishing Rules

- Publish one useful idea at a time.
- Do not promise rankings, revenue, traffic, or guaranteed customers.
- Keep the CTA soft and helpful.
- Track published links in `CAMPAIGN-PUBLISHING-TRACKER.md`.
- Reuse the same idea across platforms, but adjust the wording.

## Day 1 Ready-To-Post

### LinkedIn

A hungry customer checks your restaurant online before they visit, call, reserve, or order.

Can they quickly find:

- your menu?
- your hours?
- your location?
- your phone number?
- what makes you worth visiting?

If not, that is the first fix.

## Day 5 Ready-To-Post

Restaurant reviews are public customer service.

A good reply is usually:

- short
- calm
- thankful
- specific
- human

For serious complaints, do not argue online. Move the conversation to direct contact.

Review response templates:
`https://restaurantaibot.com/restaurant-review-response-templates.html`
