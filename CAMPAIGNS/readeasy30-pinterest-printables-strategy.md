# ReadEasy30 Pinterest Printables Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use Pinterest to attract parents, homeschool families, tutors, ESL helpers, and adult-learning helpers who search for printable reading practice.

This campaign focuses on:

- printable reading worksheets
- calm reading practice
- Bubbles mascot branding
- functional literacy practice
- no-shame reading support

## Setup checklist

1. Create a free Pinterest Business account.
2. Use ReadEasy30 branding in the profile name and description.
3. Link the profile to `https://readeasy30.com`.
4. Create 3 to 5 clean vertical pin templates in Canva.
5. Use vertical dimensions: 1000 x 1500 pixels.
6. Use calm colors: soft blues, pastel greens, light backgrounds, and Bubbles-themed accents.
7. Keep text large enough to read on a phone.
8. Avoid flashing, loud, cluttered, or panic-based design.
9. Use search-friendly descriptions.
10. Track each pin in the posting tracker.

## Safety notes

Avoid medical or diagnosis claims.

Do not claim ReadEasy30 treats dyslexia, ADHD, or any learning condition.

Use safer wording:

- calm reading practice
- useful for struggling readers
- may help learners who need less pressure
- parent/helper-supported practice
- short daily lessons
- no-shame reading practice
