## Post 2 — Parent help

Helping a reader does not have to feel stressful.

Try this simple routine:

1. Read slowly.
2. Reread one hard sentence.
3. Ask one question.
4. Find proof in the story.
5. Stop with encouragement.

## Group Posting Notes

Before posting in a Facebook group:

- Read the group rules.
- Do not post links if not allowed.
- Ask for feedback when possible.
- Do not post the same text in many groups at once.
- Be honest that the sites are being built and improved.

## Safer group angle

Instead of dropping a link first, ask:

What helps a reader practice without feeling embarrassed?

Then share ReadEasy30 only if appropriate.

## Tracking

Record date, group or page, post topic, link used, comments, and improvement ideas.
