# Education YouTube Shorts Two-Week Plan

Start: Monday, 2026-06-01

Purpose: publish 30-40 second YouTube Shorts 3 times per week for 2 weeks, then review performance before making the next batch.

Primary sites:

- ReadEasy30: https://readeasy30.com/
- MathEasy30: https://matheasy30.com/

## Rules

- 3 Shorts per week.
- 6 total Shorts before review.
- Each video should be 30-40 seconds.
- Use vertical 9:16 format.
- Use large captions.
- Speak slowly.
- Keep one clear idea per video.
- End with one call to action.
- No guaranteed-result claims.
- No shame language.
- No paid ads yet.
- No live tracking pixels yet.
- No medical, diagnosis, or special-education claims.

# Production Checklist Per Video

1. Choose the script.
2. Make vertical 9:16 video.
3. Use large captions.
4. Keep voice slow and clear.
5. Use calm background music only if it does not distract.
6. Show the site name at the end.
7. Put the main link in description.
8. Add hashtags.
9. Record post date and early results.

1. Which topic got the most views?
2. Which topic got the best engagement?
3. Which hook was strongest?
4. Did reading or math get more interest?
5. Did adult learner, parent helper, ESL, or worksheet content perform better?
6. Should the next 2 weeks focus on ReadEasy30, MathEasy30, or both?
7. Should future videos use Bubbles, screen recording, face/avatar, or simple text slides?

## Decision after review

Do not make a large next batch until the first 6 videos are reviewed.

Next batch should be based on what worked, not guesses.
