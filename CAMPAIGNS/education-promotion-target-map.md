# Education Promotion Target Map

Purpose: organize who we are trying to reach, what message fits them, and which website/page to send them to.

Primary sites:

- ReadEasy30: `https://readeasy30.com/`
- MathEasy30: `https://matheasy30.com/`

## Message rules by audience

### Parents

Use:

- “Help a reader practice without pressure.”
- “Short lessons, read aloud, and simple questions.”
- “Start where the learner is.”

Avoid:

- “Fix reading fast.”
- “Guaranteed improvement.”
- “Your child is behind.”

### Adult learners

Use:

- “Private reading practice without shame.”
- “Simple, respectful, and calm.”
- “Start where you are.”

Avoid:

- Babyish graphics.
- School-failure language.
- Anything that sounds embarrassing.

### ESL learners

Use:

- “Short English passages.”
- “Useful vocabulary.”
- “Read aloud support.”

Avoid:

- Overpromising fluency.
- Complicated grammar language.

### Math learners

Use:

- “One small math step at a time.”
- “Confidence first. Speed later.”
- “Use hints, drawings, and calm practice.”

Avoid:

- Speed-test language.
- Genius/ace-test promises.
- Panic visuals.
