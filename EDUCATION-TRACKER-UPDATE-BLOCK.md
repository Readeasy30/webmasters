## Status definitions

- `Published`: live post exists or outreach form was submitted.
- `Designed`: asset exists or was exported but not posted.
- `Drafted`: copy/script exists only.
- `Blocked`: account, access, route, or tool not ready.
- `Paused`: intentionally not using now.
- `Needs edit`: asset exists but should be fixed before posting.

## Do not track

Do not store private passwords, account recovery data, private customer data, scraped contacts, or private student data.
