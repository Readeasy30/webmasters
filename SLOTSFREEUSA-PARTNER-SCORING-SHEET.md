# SlotsFreeUSA Partner Scoring Sheet

Last updated: 2026-05-28

## Purpose

Use this sheet before listing a future partner on SlotsFreeUSA. The goal is to protect visitors, keep reviews honest, and avoid unsafe claims.

## Scoring Scale

Use 1 to 5 points.

- 1 = weak or unclear
- 2 = needs work
- 3 = acceptable
- 4 = strong
- 5 = excellent

## Rating Meaning

| Total Score | Meaning | Action |
|---:|---|---|
| 45-50 | Strong fit | Consider review after final checks |
| 35-44 | Possible fit | Needs notes and careful review |
| 25-34 | Weak fit | Do not promote without improvements |
| Under 25 | Poor fit | Do not list |

## Required Notes

Before approval, record:

- partner name
- website URL
- program or contact path
- allowed locations
- blocked locations
- age rule
- offer summary
- support page
- terms page
- disclosure needs
- tracking status
- review date

## Review Questions

1. Can a beginner understand the offer?
2. Are the terms easy to find?
3. Are location rules clear?
4. Are age rules clear?
5. Is support easy to reach?
6. Does the partner avoid risky promises?
7. Can SlotsFreeUSA describe the partner honestly?
8. Would the review still be useful without a paid link?

## Stop Conditions

Do not list or promote if:

- terms are hidden
- age rules are unclear
- location rules are unclear
- support is missing
- risky promises are required
- disclosure is not allowed
- tracking details are not approved
- privacy changes are needed but not ready
