# EDUCATION PUBLISHING SCHEDULE

Posting cadence for ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 short-form video. The goal is a
steady, sustainable rhythm you can keep up alone — three posts a week, every
week, beats ten posts one week and silence the next.

## Cadence: 3x per week — Monday / Wednesday / Friday

| Day | Slot (local) | Site focus | Notes |
|-----|--------------|------------|-------|
| **Monday** | ~7:00 AM | ReadEasy30 | Start the week with a reading tip. |
| **Wednesday** | ~7:00 AM | MathEasy30 | Midweek math confidence clip. |
| **Friday** | ~7:00 AM | Alternate / sister-site cross-promo | Whichever site you didn't lead with; occasionally promote the other site. |

Weekends are off by default. If you want to bank content, batch-record on a
weekend and schedule — but don't add weekend *posting* slots; the 3/week rhythm
is the commitment.

## Channels (post the same clip to all four)

Each video goes out to all four short-form platforms. One clip, four uploads.

1. **YouTube Shorts**
2. **TikTok**
3. **Instagram Reels**
4. **Facebook Reels**

Use the per-clip captions/titles/hashtags from
`EDUCATION-YOUTUBE-UPLOAD-METADATA.md`. Same video file works on all four
(1080×1920 vertical).

## Weekly flow

1. **Sun (optional, 20 min):** confirm the 3 clips for the week are rendered and
   in hand (`make_shorts.py` output). Drop captions into a notes file.
2. **Mon / Wed / Fri morning (~10 min each):** upload the day's clip to all four
   channels, paste caption, publish.
3. **Friday (5 min):** log what went out and any early numbers; pick next week's
   3 clips from `EDUCATION-YOUTUBE-SHORTS-BATCH-01.md`.

## Rules

- **Consistency over volume.** Three reliable posts a week is the win.
- **Always end on the site.** Every clip ends on `readeasy30.com` or
  `matheasy30.com`.
- **Don't chase virality.** Helpful + steady compounds.
- **Recycle.** After the 10-script batch is exhausted, rotate the best
  performers back in with fresh thumbnails.
