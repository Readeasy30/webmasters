# Short Video Script Template

Use this for 15 to 40 second website, Canva, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, Pinterest, and X video drafts.

## Script formula

### 1. Hook

Say the problem or benefit in one simple line.

Example:

Planning a Branson trip should not take twenty browser tabs.

### 2. Helpful promise

Say what the website helps with.

Example:

BransonBlastUSA helps you find shows, food, family fun, lake ideas, and local deals in one place.

### 3. Quick proof or example

Give two or three examples.

Example:

Start with trip ideas, local restaurant picks, or family-friendly stops.

### 4. Next step

Tell the viewer exactly what to do.

Example:

Visit BransonBlastUSA.com and start with the trip ideas section.

## Fill-in template

Hook:

[One clear visitor problem or benefit]

Promise:

[What the site helps them do]

Examples:

- [Example 2]
- [Example 3]

Next step:

[One action]

## Rules

- Use short sentences.
- Avoid hype.
- Do not promise guaranteed results.
- Do not mention affiliate partners unless approved.
- Do not use medical, financial, legal, or official educational claims without review.
