# Wholelychit Marketing Formula

Last updated: 2026-05-28

This is the reusable marketing formula for Wholelychit websites. Use it for SlotsFreeUSA first, then adapt it for ReadEasy30, MathEasy30, PetNeeds.ai, Restaurants.ai, and future sites.

## Core Formula

**Traffic + Trust + Simple Offer + Follow-Up = Revenue Opportunity**

Every website needs all four parts:

1. **Traffic** — people find the site through search, social, video, referrals, or local discovery.
2. **Trust** — visitors quickly understand the site, who it helps, and why it is safe to use.
3. **Simple Offer** — the visitor sees one clear next step, such as play free, read a guide, compare options, join a list, or contact us.
4. **Follow-Up** — the site captures future value through email, retargetable content, repeat visits, social follows, or saved resources.

If one part is missing, revenue is weaker.

## The 7-Step Website Growth Loop

Use this loop every week.

### 1. Pick one audience

Do not market to everyone.

Define one visitor group:

- beginner
- parent/helper
- pet owner
- local business owner
- casual entertainment visitor
- buyer researching a product or service

### 2. Pick one problem

One page should solve one main problem.

Examples:

- SlotsFreeUSA: “I want to try free slots without risking money.”
- ReadEasy30: “My child needs calm reading practice.”
- PetNeeds.ai: “I need a simple checklist for a new pet.”
- Restaurants.ai: “My restaurant needs more local customers.”

### 3. Create one helpful page

The page should include:

- clear title
- quick answer near the top
- simple explanation
- step-by-step help
- common mistakes
- safe next step
- internal links
- disclosure or safety note when needed

### 4. Build one trust asset

Trust assets include:

- About page
- How we review page
- Safety page
- Terms page
- Privacy page
- Disclosure page
- FAQ page
- checklist page
- comparison table
- transparent scoring system

### 5. Add one simple conversion

Choose one conversion goal per page:

- play the free tool
- read the beginner guide
- compare options
- join email list later
- contact for partner/ad inquiry
- save a checklist
- click an approved partner link later

### 6. Repurpose the page

Turn one page into:

- 1 short video script
- 1 Facebook post
- 1 Pinterest pin idea
- 1 LinkedIn post if business angle fits
- 1 Reddit discussion prompt without spam
- 1 email/newsletter note after email setup is ready

### 7. Improve monthly

Every month:

1. Check which pages exist.
2. Add internal links.
3. Improve weak titles.
4. Add FAQs.
5. Add comparison tables.
6. Remove stale or risky claims.
7. Add new pages based on the strongest topic.

## Page Quality Formula

**Clear + Useful + Safe + Fast + Next Step**

A page is not ready unless it is:

- clear in the first 5 seconds
- useful without needing another site
- safe and honest
- fast on mobile
- connected to one next step

## Revenue Path Formula

Build revenue in this order:

1. **Audience trust** — useful pages, no hype, clear rules.
2. **Traffic base** — SEO pages and social repurposing.
3. **Soft monetization** — display ad placeholders, sponsor pages, partner inquiry page.
4. **Verified partners** — approved affiliate programs only.
5. **Tracking and compliance** — affiliate tracking, FTC disclosure, privacy updates.
6. **Optimization** — compare clicks, page views, signups, and content winners.

Do not add live ads, live tracking, or live affiliate links until approval, privacy, disclosure, and account rules are ready.

## Website Launch Checklist

Before pushing a site harder:

- Homepage says what the site does.
- One main call to action is obvious.
- Mobile menu works.
- Footer links work.
- Privacy, Terms, Contact, and Disclosure pages exist.
- Sitemap and robots files exist.
- No private keys or tokens are in the repo.
- No risky earnings claims.
- No unsupported health, legal, financial, or gambling claims.
- No live affiliate links without disclosure and verification.
- No live tracking without privacy setup.

## Standard Weekly Work Batch

Each week, create:

1. one website page or major page improvement
2. one trust improvement
3. one internal-link improvement
4. one social/video batch
5. one status update

This keeps the websites moving without scattered work.
