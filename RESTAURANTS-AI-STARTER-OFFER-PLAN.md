# Restaurants.ai / RestaurantAIbot Starter Offer Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Naming Rule

Use `Restaurants.ai` for the live website/domain spelling.

Use `RestaurantAIbot` as the product/service name only when describing the AI-assisted restaurant growth helper.

Do not use older spellings:

- reasturant.ai
- restaurant.ai
- restaurant.ai

## Purpose

Use this plan to make the restaurant project clear, useful, and easy for a busy restaurant owner to understand fast.

The first goal is not complicated software.

The first goal is a simple offer:

**Help local restaurants get found, look better online, and bring customers back with practical AI-assisted marketing.**

## Target Audience

- independent restaurants
- family restaurants
- food trucks
- cafes
- diners
- takeout restaurants
- small local chains
- restaurants with weak websites or inconsistent social posts

## Main Pain Points

Restaurant owners often need help with:

1. slow days
2. weak Google Business Profile content
3. poor menu descriptions
4. outdated website copy
5. no social posting rhythm
6. few review responses
7. bad mobile first impression
8. no simple local marketing plan
9. not enough repeat customers
10. too many tools and not enough time

## Core Offer Message

RestaurantAIbot helps local restaurants use simple AI-assisted marketing to look better online, get found faster, and bring more customers back.

### Not A Fit Yet
