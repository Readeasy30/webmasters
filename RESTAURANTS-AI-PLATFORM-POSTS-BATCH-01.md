## Post 2: Menu Copy Before and After

### LinkedIn

Menu descriptions matter.

Weak:

“Chicken sandwich”

Better:

“Crispy chicken sandwich with house slaw, pickles, and spicy mayo.”

Good menu copy does not need to be fancy. It needs to help customers picture the food and decide faster.

See Restaurant Menu Copy Tips:
`https://restaurantaibot.com/restaurant-menu-copy-tips.html`

### Facebook

## Post 3: Review Replies

### LinkedIn

Restaurant reviews are public customer service.

A good reply is usually:

- short
- calm
- thankful
- specific
- human

For serious complaints, do not argue online. Move the conversation to direct contact.

Review response templates:
`https://restaurantaibot.com/restaurant-review-response-templates.html`

### Facebook

Reviews need human replies.

Thank the customer. Answer the point. Stay calm.

For serious issues, invite them to contact the restaurant directly.
