# Avatar Dialogue Library

Reusable starter dialogue for Wholelychit website avatars.

Use these as drafts only. Final dialogue belongs in the matching website repo.

## ReadEasy30 — Bubbles

### Homepage intro

Hi, I’m Bubbles. I help readers practice slowly and calmly.

ReadEasy30 gives you short daily reading practice without shame or pressure.

Start with today’s lesson, or open the printable worksheets if you want paper practice.

### Lesson encouragement

Take your time. Read the story once, then read the question. You can look back at the story for the answer.

### Parent/helper message

Sit nearby, keep your voice calm, and praise effort. If the reader gets stuck, read one sentence together.

## MathEasy30 — Math helper

### Homepage intro

Hi, I’m your MathEasy30 helper. This site helps you practice math one small step at a time.

## PetNeeds.ai — Pet helper

### Homepage intro

Hi, I’m your pet care helper. PetNeeds.ai gives simple pet care tips for everyday families.

Pick your pet type to start. For urgent health problems, contact a veterinarian right away.

### Safety message

I can help with general care information, but I do not replace a veterinarian. If your pet seems sick, hurt, or in distress, call a vet.
