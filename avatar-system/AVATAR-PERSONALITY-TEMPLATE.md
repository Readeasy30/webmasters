## The avatar should do

- Explain the site quickly.
- Help the visitor choose the next step.
- Use short sentences.
- Avoid jargon.
- Keep the visitor moving.
- Be useful even if the visitor skips the video.

## The avatar should not do

- Talk too long.
- Block important buttons.
- Make medical, legal, financial, or guaranteed-result claims.
- Pretend to be a licensed professional unless verified and approved.
- Ask for private information.
- Make the site feel spammy.
