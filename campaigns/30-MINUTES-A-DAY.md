# 30 Minutes A Day Campaign

## Core Positioning

- 30 minutes a day still counts.
- One lesson at a time.
- Slow progress is still progress.
- Confidence before speed.

## Emotional Goal

Users should feel:

"I can do this."

## Primary Platforms

- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook
- Pinterest

## Content Direction

Keep all content:

- calm
- simple
- encouraging
- practical

## First Video Ideas

1. A calm 30 minutes can change confidence.
2. Math does not need to feel scary.
3. Reading slowly is okay.
4. Small wins matter.
5. One lesson today still counts.
