# Outreach Submission Log Batch 01: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30

Last updated: 2026-06-02

This file prevents repeated outreach and keeps the first prospect batch organized.

## Rules

- Record only actions that were actually completed.
- Do not mark anything sent unless the form or email was really submitted.
- Send one route at a time.
- Do not mass-send.
- One follow-up only after 7 to 10 days, if appropriate.
- Stop on opt-out, no-contact request, bounce, or no response after one follow-up.

## Status key

- Ready = copy prepared, not sent yet.
- Submitted = form or message sent.
- Follow-up due = one follow-up may be sent after 7 to 10 days.
- Follow-up sent = one follow-up sent.
- Stop = no further contact.
- Blocked = route did not work.
