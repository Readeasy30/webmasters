# Wholelychit 5-Site Marketing Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

This is the shared marketing plan for five active Wholelychit projects:

1. SlotsFreeUSA
2. RestaurantAIbot / Restaurants.ai
3. ReadEasy30
4. MathEasy30
5. PetNeeds.ai

The goal is to build traffic, trust, useful content, and future revenue without adding unsafe live ads, tracking, payment systems, private keys, public AI tools, user accounts, uploads, or affiliate links before approval.

## Big Strategy

Build each website as a helpful niche resource first.

Revenue comes later from trust.

Core formula:

**Helpful page + clear audience + simple next step + trust signal + repeat publishing = long-term traffic and revenue.**

## Cross-Site Promotion Rules

### Education pair

ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 should link to each other as sister sites.

Suggested text:

> Need math practice too? Try MathEasy30, our calm sister practice site.

> Need reading practice too? Try ReadEasy30, our calm sister practice site.

### PetNeeds.ai to education sites

Only cross-promote when family/child context makes sense.

Example:

> Helping children build daily habits? ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 can help with calm learning routines.

### RestaurantAIbot to other sites

Do not force consumer-site cross-promotion. RestaurantAIbot should stay focused on restaurant owners.

### SlotsFreeUSA

Do not cross-promote family/education/pet content from SlotsFreeUSA. Keep adult entertainment separate.
