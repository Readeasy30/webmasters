# PetNeeds.ai SEO Article Queue

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

This file controls the first PetNeeds.ai SEO article batch. The goal is practical, safe, plain-English pet help that can become search traffic.

## Article Rules

Every article should include:

- clear title
- short quick answer
- practical steps
- what to watch for
- when to call a veterinarian
- FAQ section
- internal links
- date reviewed/updated
- no diagnosis
- no medication dosing
- no treatment promises

## Standard Article CTA

Use this at the end of helpful non-emergency pages:

> Want a simple pet-care starter list? Visit PetNeeds.ai for beginner pet checklists, safety tips, and practical pet-owner guides.

For urgent/safety pages, use this instead:

> This page gives general pet-care information. It does not replace a veterinarian. If symptoms are serious, sudden, painful, or getting worse, call your veterinarian or emergency pet clinic.

## Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing, confirm:

- The article answers one clear question.
- The first screen gives useful help.
- Safety guidance is clear.
- The article does not pretend to diagnose.
- The article does not recommend medication doses.
- The affiliate/product area is disclosure-ready or category-only.
- There are at least 2 internal links.
- The meta description is written.
- The social post and Canva prompt are planned.
