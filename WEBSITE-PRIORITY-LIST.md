## Rule for changing priority

Change priority only when:

- a site is blocked,
- a site has a near-term launch opportunity,
- a safety/compliance issue appears,
- a monetization opportunity becomes urgent,
- Gerry clearly changes business focus.

Otherwise, follow the order above.
